About Eco-Search Engines

Eco-search engines like Ecosia, OceanHero, and Ekoru use their ad revenue to fund environmental projects. Ecosia plants trees, OceanHero removes ocean plastic, and Ekoru supports marine conservation. By switching your default search engine, you can make a positive impact with every search.
